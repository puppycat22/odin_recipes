Margarita

A classic cocktail

Easy to make cocktail to please any guest this summer!

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces of white tequila
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces of triple sec
  • 1 fluid ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of coarse sea salt
  • 1 lime wedge

Instructions

  1. Moisten the rim of a glass with a lime wedge. Sprinkle salt onto a plate. Lightly dip the moistened rim into the salt.
  2. Place a large ice cube in the glass and freeze the prepared glass until ready to serve.
  3. Fill a cocktail shaker with fresh ice. Add tequila, triple sec, and lime juice. Cover and shake vigorously until the outside of the shaker has frosted.
  4. Strain margarita into the chilled glass and garnish with a slice of lime.
